Article 1. Establishment of the Executive Council of the International
Financial Center in Vietnam (hereinafter referred to as the “Executive
Council”), comprising:
1. President of
the Executive Council: Permanent Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Hoa Binh.
2. Vice Presidents of
the Executive Council:
- Minister of Finance
Nguyen Van Thang
- Governor of the State
Bank of Vietnam Nguyen Thi Hong;
- Chairperson of the
People's Committee of Ho Chi Minh City Nguyen Van Duoc;
- Chairperson of the
People's Committee of Nang City Pham Duc An.
3. Members of the
Executive Council who are leaders of relevant ministries and ministerial
agencies:
- Deputy Minister of
Justice Nguyen Thanh Tinh;

- Deputy Minister of
Industry and Trade Nguyen Sinh Nhat Tan;
- Deputy Minister of
Agriculture and Environment Nguyen Hoang Hiep.
Article 2. Functions, tasks, and powers of the Executive Council:
1. Use their own seal
with the National emblem; assign the assisting body to manage the seal of the
Executive Council in accordance with regulations.
2. Promulgate
strategies, roadmaps, and development plans of the International Financial
Center in Vietnam (hereinafter referred to as the “International Financial
Center”) based on the conditions, potential, and development needs of the
International Financial Centers in Ho Chi Minh City and Da Nang City.
3. Promulgate
operational regulations for the International Financial Center upon proposals
of the Executive body of the International Financial Center in Ho Chi Minh City
and the Executive body of the International Financial Center in Da Nang City
(hereinafter referred to as “Executive bodies").
4. Give opinions on the
appointment, reappointment, and dismissal Presidents of Executive bodies.
5. Decide on unsettled
matters between 02 Executive bodies.
6. Establish the
advisory council of the International Financial Center comprising reputable
domestic and international financial and legal experts, which has the function
of providing consultancy and advice to the Executive Council on developing the
International Financial Center;
...
...

8. Periodically, when
necessary to address issues ultra vires, or upon request, the Executive Council
shall report on the construction, operation, and development of the
International Financial Center to propose policies and seek guidance from the
Government or the Prime Minister.
9. Perform other tasks
related to the International Financial Center as assigned by the Government and
the Prime Minister.
Article 3. This Decision comes into force from the day on which it is
signed.
The President of
the Executive Council of the International Financial Center in Vietnam,
Ministers, Heads of ministerial-level agencies, Heads of Governmental agencies,
Chairpersons of the People's Committees of provinces and central-affiliated
cities, heads of relevant agencies, organizations, individuals, and members
listed in Article 1 shall implement this Decision./.
